Rule1_ExposedToRiskback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the risk a device is exposed to based on its incidents.

Incident c(?i) , involves device op(?i,?d) , caused by risk op(?i,?r) -> exposed to risk op(?d,?r)

Rule2_ActionAffectsback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers which devices are affected by a corrective action.

Corrective Action c(?c) , resolves incident op(?c,?i) , involves device op(?i,?d) -> action affects device op(?c,?d)

Rule3_ManufacturedInback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the manufacturing country of a device based on its manufacturer.

Medical Device c(?d) , has manufacturer op(?d,?m) , located in op(?m,?c) -> manufactured in op(?d,?c)
